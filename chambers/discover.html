Discover Lilongwe

Lilongwe Old town

Lilongwe old town. This is the main Central Business District in lilongwe. It is a busy area where business is the main activity.

Business Center- The City Center

Remarkable place to do business. You will find shops and offices to rent and connect to other businesses.

Flat fertile farm land.

If you are into farming business, the land is just remarkable. Flat and fetile land ideal for large and small scale farming business.

Kamuzu Mausoleum

Visit the Mausoleum of the first president of Malawi, late Dr. Hastings Kamuzu Banda in city center. Learn more about the History of the one who was taken as the life president of Malawi.

Discover Lake Malawi

The lake full of fresh water. Enjoy the Chambo of lake Malawi. If you are in the tourism sector business, the lake is a good place for you to invest your money. Visit spectacular beaches along the lake

Spectacular view of Mulanje Mountain

See neighboring countries by standing at Mulanje mountain, the largest mountain in the southern east of Africa. View the green scenery of tea grown at the base of the mountain
