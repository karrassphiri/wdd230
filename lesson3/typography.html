Heading Font: Montserrat

Montserrat is versatile and has wide range of weight. It comes in 36 different styles, and it's an idea font for almost anything.

Paragraph Font: Lato

It is a Sans serif font which has become most popular as it is very clean and elegant and not too distracting. It is available in all operating systems.
